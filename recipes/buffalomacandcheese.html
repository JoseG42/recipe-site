Buffalo Mac and Cheese

This spicy twist on classic mac and cheese is sure to be a hit!

No I'm not writing another blurb here. It's only funny the first time.

Description

This Buffalo Mac and Cheese is creamy, cheesy, and has just the right amount of heat. It's the perfect comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Stir in the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper; cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk, cooking until the mixture thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the buffalo sauce and cheddar cheese until melted.
  6. Add the cooked macaroni and sour cream; mix until well combined.
  7. Transfer to a baking dish and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes.
