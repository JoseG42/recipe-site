Banana Bread

Everyday is the same. wake up, eat, work, sleep, repeat.

Today was much the same. Woke up, had a microwave breakfast sandwich, forty minute commute to work, said hello to sandra like usual. There it was in her smile, in her eyes, all over her face. She had fallen in love. I was happy for her, but also sad. I wanted to be the one she shared her life with, but I was not. "What's got you smilling so bright today?" I asked her. "Alex brought in banana bread" she said, pointing to the break room. "BANANA BREAD? AT WORK? OH YEAH!"
